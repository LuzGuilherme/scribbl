# Blog Article Templates

Structure: **Captivating title → Strong intro → Scannable body → Clear CTA**

## Template 1: How-To Guide

**Title format:**
- "How to [achieve outcome] in [timeframe]"
- "[Number] Steps to [desired result]"
- "The Simple Way to [do thing]"

**Structure:**

```markdown
# [Title]

[Opening hook: Why this matters]

[Preview what they'll learn]

## Step 1: [Action-oriented heading]

[Explanation in 2-3 short paragraphs]

**Why this matters:** [Brief benefit]

## Step 2: [Action-oriented heading]

[Repeat pattern]

## Common mistakes to avoid

- **[Mistake 1]:** [Why it's bad and how to avoid it]
- **[Mistake 2]:** [Why it's bad and how to avoid it]

## What's next

[Summary and next steps]

[CTA: Try product/feature or read related article]
```

## Writing Guidelines

### Titles (SEO-Friendly)

Include:
- Target keyword naturally
- Specific benefit or outcome
- Number (if relevant)

**Examples:**
- "How to Automate Reports Without Coding (Save 3 Hours/Week)"
- "Why Manual Reporting Fails (And What Works Instead)"
- "Best Project Management Tools for Solo Founders in 2024"

### Introduction

**First paragraph:** Hook (problem, stat, bold claim, question)
**Second paragraph:** What they'll learn
**Third paragraph:** Optional credibility

**Keep it under 150 words total.**

### Body Content

**Paragraphs:** 2-4 sentences max
**Subheadings:** Every 200-300 words
**Lists:** Use liberally
**Examples:** Include real scenarios

### SEO Optimization

Must-haves:
- Target keyword in title, H2s, naturally throughout
- Meta description (150-160 characters)
- Alt text for images
- Internal links (2-3)
- External links (2-3)

### Conclusion

1. Recap key point (1 sentence)
2. What they gained (1-2 sentences)
3. Clear next step (CTA)

## Article Length Guidelines

- How-to guides: 1,000-1,500 words
- Problem-solution: 800-1,200 words
- Comparison posts: 1,500-2,000 words

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Weak intro:** "In today's world..."
✅ **Strong hook:** Lead with problem or result

❌ **Dense paragraphs:** 6+ line blocks
✅ **Short paragraphs:** 2-4 sentences

❌ **No structure:** Wall of text
✅ **Scannable:** H2s every 200-300 words

❌ **Vague advice:** "Improve your process"
✅ **Specific steps:** "Run reports at 9am Friday"
