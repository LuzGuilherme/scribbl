## How-To Guide Template

**Best for:** Informational queries, tutorials, step-by-step processes
**Typical length:** 1,500-3,000 words
**Target featured snippet:** Yes (numbered steps)

```markdown
# How to [Achieve Result]: [Number] [Adjective] Steps

[Opening paragraph: State the problem, promise the solution, include primary keyword]

In this guide, you'll learn:
- [Key outcome 1]
- [Key outcome 2]
- [Key outcome 3]

## What is [Topic]? (Optional - if definition needed)

[Brief explanation for context]

## Why [Topic] Matters

[2-3 paragraphs on importance/benefits]

## How to [Main Topic]: Step-by-Step

### Step 1: [Action Verb + Specific Task]

[Explanation of step]

**Pro tip:** [Additional insight]

### Step 2: [Action Verb + Specific Task]

[Explanation of step]

[Image/screenshot if applicable]

### Step 3: [Action Verb + Specific Task]

[Continue pattern...]

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- **Mistake 1:** [Description and how to avoid]
- **Mistake 2:** [Description and how to avoid]
- **Mistake 3:** [Description and how to avoid]

## [Topic] Best Practices

[Additional tips and recommendations]

## Frequently Asked Questions

### [Question 1 - from PAA]?
[Direct answer]

### [Question 2 - from PAA]?
[Direct answer]

## Conclusion

[Summary of key steps, restate benefit, CTA]

**Next steps:** [What reader should do now]
```

## Listicle Template

**Best for:** "Best of" queries, tool roundups, tip collections
**Typical length:** 1,500-4,000 words
**Target featured snippet:** Yes (bulleted list)

```markdown
# [Number] Best [Topic] for [Audience/Use Case] in [Year]

[Opening: Why this matters, what you'll learn]

**Quick picks:**
- **Best overall:** [Item] - [One-line reason]
- **Best for beginners:** [Item] - [One-line reason]
- **Best value:** [Item] - [One-line reason]

## How We Chose These [Items]

[Brief methodology: criteria used, testing process, expertise]

## The [Number] Best [Topic]

### 1. [Item Name] - Best for [Specific Use]

![Image of item]

[2-3 paragraph description]

**Pros:**
- [Pro 1]
- [Pro 2]
- [Pro 3]

**Cons:**
- [Con 1]
- [Con 2]

**Pricing:** [Details]

**Best for:** [Specific audience/use case]

[CTA button/link if affiliate]

---

### 2. [Item Name] - Best for [Specific Use]

[Repeat pattern...]

---

[Continue for all items...]

## Comparison Table

| Feature | Item 1 | Item 2 | Item 3 |
|---------|--------|--------|--------|
| Price | $ | $$ | $$$ |
| Feature A | ✓ | ✓ | ✗ |
| Feature B | ✗ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Best for | X users | Y users | Z users |

## How to Choose the Right [Item]

[Guidance on decision-making factors]

## Frequently Asked Questions

### [Relevant question]?
[Answer]

## Final Verdict

[Summary recommendation based on reader type]

**Our top pick:** [Item] because [reason]
```

**Typical length:** 400-800 words

```markdown
# [News Event]: [Key Detail] - [What It Means]

**Last updated:** [Date and time]

[One-paragraph summary of the news with key facts]

## What Happened

[Detailed explanation of the news/update]

## Why This Matters

[Impact and significance]

## Key Details

- [Detail 1]
- [Detail 2]
- [Detail 3]

## What's Next

[Expected developments, timeline]

## Our Take

[Brief analysis/commentary - demonstrates expertise]

---

*This story is developing. Check back for updates.*
```
